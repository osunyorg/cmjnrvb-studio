The School of Decorative Arts of Paris is opening a consultation and is looking for a creative studio to support them in the future artistic direction of its website. The studio is selected in order to rethink the user experience and embody the visual identity created by the Collectif Much.
The project was designed for a modular, scalable, accessible and eco-designed design. A system design was therefore deployed in order to respond to the various challenges of the project.

Thanks for Anne-Sophie Krebs, Jérôme Meudic, Amélie Pauvert and Laurent Doucelance from Ensad's Team for their trust.
