Forland is a collaborative digital platform dedicated to sustainable land management. It is designed to support local decision makers in territorial planning, integrating environmental, economic and social data — while fostering a participatory and contextual approach. It allows you to visualize a territory in real time, evaluate the resources present, simulate different scenarios of use or restoration, and analyse soil changes.

The studio accompanies the Forland team to create its visual identity: logotype, graphic charter, presentation brochure, website and product.

Printing of two-tone brochures by Impro Montreuil.
