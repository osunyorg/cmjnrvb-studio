Following the desire to completely recast the mobile app on UX, interface design and technical aspects, the studio collaborates with the mobile team on design aspects. Takumi is part of the Mediapart team and implements the application design. It will propose a global harmonisation of the identity attributes of the journal in order to clarify the foundations of the graphic charter. We use the Barcelona illustrator Miguel Porlan to illustrate the app's home screens as well as the auto-promos inviting readers to subscribe, share or participate.
Discover here work on the identity of the newspaper.
