A new project that addresses human rights and freedoms issues from an innovative cultural and creative perspective, led by Euromed France Network, the Education League - Fédération des Bouches-du-Rhône, The Cairo Institute for Human Rights Studies (CIHRS) and Digital and Poetic Video Instants. The project Taethir"Influence" in Arabic, aims to promote a culture of human rights in Mediterranean countries by building on influential and innovative digital and artistic practices.

From censorship to artistic outreach was adopted to embody a strong, committed and accessible visual identity. A logotype that combines Arabic and Latin writing using digital age codes (glitch, pixel, network connection...). The studio realizes the visual identity accompanied by various templates: administrative documents, stationery, instagram, powerpoint...
