The Villa Medici, perched on the side of Pincio in Rome, is one of the noblest sentinels of the Italian Renaissance. Former residence of the Medici, it displays its light facades above the city, like a showcase suspended between sculpted gardens and Roman light. Since the 17th century, it has welcomed artists and creators in residence, continuing a tradition of excellence where history and invention interact. A place of memory as much as a laboratory of ideas, the Villa Médicis remains today a cultural beacon: a refuge where art is refined, dreamed and reinvented.

In 2023, the studio responds jointly with the developers of Noesya (external link) to the call for tenders and offers an artistic direction in harmony with the visual identity created by the Italian studio Bahut, as well as the use of the opens source Osuny CMS (external link).
