The High Authority for the Transparency of Public Life is a French independent administrative authority created as a result of Cahuzac at the end of 2013. Its mission is to receive, monitor and with the tax administration and publish status declarations and statements by senior officials, Governments. In 2015, the HATVP wants to renew its website and the studio takes care of the global overhaul: UX, design, development.
Team
Takumi Kobayashi: Artistic direction, webdesign and UX
Frédéric Fornini: Artistic direction of illustrations
Jérôme Montagne : Front-end development
Alice Loeser : Back-end development
