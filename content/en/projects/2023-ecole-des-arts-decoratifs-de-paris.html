Under the direction of Emmanuel Tibloux , director of the School of Decorative Arts of Paris, the studio supports his team on the redesign of the website during the year 2023. Since the global design, the studio has been rethinking the paths user-ices, information architecture, global navigation and an artistic direction embodying the created visual identity by the Collective Many. A design system is developed to to imagine a modular site and to install the foundations of the digital ecosystem of the school.

Family of character Bascule was designed specifically by the collective Beaucoup.
Technical realization & development by agency_advency

Thanks Anne-Sophie Krebs, Jérôme Meudic, Amélie Pauvert and Laurent Doucelance of Ensad's team for their trust.
