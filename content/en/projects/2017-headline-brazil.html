Headline is an independent Brazilian media based in São Paulo, which stands out for its innovative approach to journalism. Its ambition is to produce quality content on politics, culture and future issues, while offering independent journalists and small editors the digital tools and infrastructures usually reserved for large media. Through recommendation technologies, monetization systems and dissemination solutions, Headline enables these content creators to gain visibility and economic autonomy.

Founded by journalists with experience in Brazil and abroad, the media responds to a simple observation: many talents work independently, but lack technical and financial support to reach a broad audience. By combining journalistic production and technology platform, Headline thus embodies a hybrid model that feeds both the public debate and the ecosystem of independent journalism in Brazil.

Andrei Netto, journalist and co-founder of the project, called on the studio to accompany him on the first phase of the project: to produce an MVP version of the website. The studio works on identity, UX and interface design aspects.

Team
Julien Mehl, Project Manager
Jérémy Maurel and Takumi Kobayashi, designers
