The issue Open air, produced by Mediapart, is a series of free online video debates aimed at deciphering news and major societal issues. Each episode features mediapart journalists, sometimes live and in public, with guests - specialists, political actors or associations - to analyse a topic (discrimination, politics, geopolitics, etc.), provide critical insights and stimulate collective reflection.

Visual identity of the program. Creation of the logotype, reflection with the team : Valentine Oberti, Mathieu Magnaudeix, Christophe Gueugneau and Edwy Plenel.
