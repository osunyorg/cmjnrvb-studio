The City of Science and Industry, at the heart of the Parc de la Villette in Paris, is a vast contemporary temple dedicated to the Human curiosity. Designed as a bridge between research and culture and the general public, it unfolds its luminous and geometric spaces around the same desire: to make knowledge alive.

Place of transmission as much as contemplation, the Cité des sciences embodies a generous vision of knowledge: a space where one learns in amazement, where pedagogy becomes a journey, and where every visitor - child, dreamer, researcher - find something to nourish his thirst for infinite.

The Spintank agency uses the studio to propose a new artistic and interactive direction.
