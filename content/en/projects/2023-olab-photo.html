Olab is a laboratory-boutique-galerie marseillaise valuing the practice of silver photography, it is a place where the amateur-ices and professional-they can come develop their films and print their photographs. It is a place of exhibition and warm meetings around this practice.

The Founder Damien Chamcirkan appeals to the studio to accompany him on the artistic direction of its communication media: stationery, rate grid, posters, video animations... Collaboration is a red wire since 2023. Carefully printed by Éric de La Platinum in Marseille on Fedrigoni paper and metal pantone. Logotype designed by Guigui Smalls
