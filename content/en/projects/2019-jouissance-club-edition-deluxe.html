The book Jouissance Club : A mapping of the pleasure of Jüne Plã is a sex education guide open to all orientations and identities. He proposes to leave the focus on penetration to explore many other ways to experience and offer pleasure. Through illustrated diagrams, maps of erogenous zones and descriptions of movements or situations, the book encourages a caring, decomplexed and diversified approach to sexuality.

Jüne uses the studio to design the deluxe edition of the book. It is an expanded version of the version original, more colorful and more incarnate, with bonus schemes Anatomical zones of pleasure. Cardboard cover and printing In direct tone, the book was reprinted a second time. All illustrations are the creation of her author Jüne Plã.
