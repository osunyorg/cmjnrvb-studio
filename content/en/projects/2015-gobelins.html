The school of Gobelins wishing to renew its image on the digital media uses a team of school elders to this recast. The school offers a catalogue of more than 1000 courses, all in the disciplines of visual creation. The complexity was to make accessible and facilitate the search for its training on All terminals. Estelle Broihanne animates and accompanies the strategic part and user experience (in pairs with Vincent De Laat). Working on the graphical identity and the interface design of the site.
You will find a proposal for an icon system for the departments of the school.
