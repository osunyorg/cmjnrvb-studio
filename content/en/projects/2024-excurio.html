Excurio is a French company specialized in creating immersive cultural experiences in virtual reality. Founded in 2005 under the name Emissive and renamed Excurio in 2024, it designs "Immersive Expeditions" allowing groups of visitors to freely explore large-scale historical or artistic universes, such as The Horizon of Kheops. Based in Paris, Excurio combines technological innovation, pedagogy and culture to make heritage accessible to the general public through collective and spectacular experiences.

The studio cmjnrvb collaborates with the source agency (advice) and Deadwater (development) to imagine their new window. We accompany excurio in artistic direction, UX and interface design.
